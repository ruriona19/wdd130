AUNT DIMEL IN THE KITCHEN

COOKIES & CREAM STRAWBERRY PARFAIT

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 (5.3-ounce) Greek Yogurt
  • 4 chocolate sandwich cookies, divided
  • 6 strawberries, hulled and diced

STEPS

  1. In a storage bag, add 3 chocolate sandwich cookies.
  2. Roll a baking pin over the storage bag to crumble the cookies.
  3. Layer half of the Greek yogurt to the bottom of a small glass.
  4. Add strawberries and another small layer of yogurt.
  5. Add the cookie crumbles, then the rest of the yogurt, and top with the remaining strawberries and a cookie.

SWEET SUMMER SMOOTHIE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup of Greek Yogurt
  • 1/2 cup frozen strawberries, unsweetened
  • 1/2 medium banana, peeled
  • 1/2 cup of low-fat milk
  • If using fresh fruit add 2/3 cup ice to the recipe

STEPS

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender
  2. Process until smooth and serve immediately

CHOCOLATE MUDSLIDE SMOOTHIE

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup ice
  • 2 cups Greek Yogurt
  • 5 tablespoons chocolate sauce
  • 2 ounces Irish cream (optional)
  • Garnish: Chocolate sauce Cocoa nibs or chocolate chips

STEPS

  1. Add chocolate sauce to the inside wall of 2 tall glasses.
  2. Place into the freezer.
  3. Combine ice, yogurt, Irish cream and chocolate sauce in a blender and blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into prepared glasses.
  5. Top with extra yogurt, chocolate sauce, and cocoa nibs.
